About the Book

Written for the woman who carries more than shows.

This book is for the woman who looks calm on the outside and yet knows that something inside has been quietly disturbed. The woman who kept functioning, kept showing up, kept speaking gently. And still knew that beneath all that competence there was a hum she could not ignore.


Seventeen chapters. Science-anchored. Warm where warmth is needed. Firm where clarity matters. Honest. Grounded. Written for you.

About the Author

Lara Sorelle

Lara Sorelle is a writer whose work explores emotional clarity, boundaries and the quiet return to self.


Her writing sits in the space between self-knowledge and self-compassion. She writes for the woman who is already doing the work and simply needs language for what she is carrying.


The Hum Beneath the Peace is her first book. The companion journal, Resolved, follows later in 2026.
