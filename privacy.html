Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy explains how larasorelle.com collects, uses and protects your personal information. It applies to all visitors to this website and anyone who subscribes to the reader list.

Who we are

This website is operated by Lara Sorelle, an independent author based in the United Kingdom. You can contact us at hello@larasorelle.com.

What information we collect

We collect your email address when you choose to join the reader list. We do not collect any other personal information through this website.

When you visit this website, standard technical information may be collected automatically, including your IP address, browser type and pages visited. This is collected through Google Search Console for the purpose of understanding how people find and use the site.

How we use your information

Your email address is used only to send you occasional updates about books, the journal and related writing. We will not send you unsolicited marketing or share your email address with anyone else.

Website usage data collected through Google Search Console is used only to improve the site and understand visitor behaviour. It is processed by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.

Your legal rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, correct any inaccurate information, request deletion of your information, and withdraw your consent at any time.

You can unsubscribe from the reader list at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email you receive from us. To exercise any other rights, please email hello@larasorelle.com.

How we store your information

Your email address is stored securely through Mailchimp, our email service provider. Mailchimp is based in the United States and processes data in accordance with UK GDPR requirements. You can read their privacy policy at mailchimp.com.

We do not store your email address on our own servers and we do not use it for any purpose beyond sending you the updates you signed up for.

Cookies

This website uses a small number of cookies. A cookie is a small file stored on your browser that helps the site function correctly.

We use a cookie to remember that you have accepted this cookie notice, so it does not appear on every visit. Google Search Console may also set cookies to collect anonymised data about site usage.

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using this website.

Third party links

This website contains links to Amazon, Etsy, Goodreads and other external websites. Once you leave larasorelle.com we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please email hello@larasorelle.com.
